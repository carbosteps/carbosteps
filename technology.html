The problem we solve

Conventional footwear wasn't designed to last.

Traditional insoles and midsoles are made from EVA or polyurethane foam — materials that compress and degrade rapidly under regular use. Within weeks, the cushioning that made a shoe feel comfortable is largely gone, yet the shoe looks intact.

CarboSteps replaces this disposable approach with a durable, structurally engineered core that maintains its performance characteristics over time — protecting joints, reducing muscle strain, and delivering consistent comfort from day one to year one.

Applications

From insoles to complete footwear.

The CarboSteps platform is designed to be versatile. Our technology is being developed across two product lines: high-performance comfort insoles that retrofit into existing footwear, and a full shoe line where the carbon architecture is integrated into the midsole from the ground up.

Both product lines share the same core technology — giving every customer access to the same engineering, regardless of how they choose to experience it.

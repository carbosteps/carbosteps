Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

At CARBOSTEPS, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website and sign up to receive updates about our products.

We only collect your email address. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

1. Information We Collect

When you sign up on our website, we collect the following information:

  • Your email address
  • The date and time of your registration
  • Basic browsing data (browser type, pages visited) via analytics tools

We do not collect sensitive personal data such as payment information, physical addresses, or government-issued identification numbers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely for the following purposes:

  • To send you product launch announcements and updates about CARBOSTEPS
  • To notify you of exclusive early access opportunities
  • To improve our website and user experience

We will never use your email for purposes other than those listed above without your explicit consent.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your email address is stored securely using industry-standard encryption practices. We use trusted third-party email service providers to manage our mailing list. These providers are contractually obligated to keep your data confidential and secure.

We retain your data for as long as you remain subscribed. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data at any time
  • Unsubscribe from our communications at any time via the unsubscribe link in any email we send

5. Cookies

Our website may use basic cookies to ensure proper functionality and to understand how visitors interact with our pages. These cookies do not track you across other websites and do not contain personally identifiable information.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time without affecting your ability to browse our website.

6. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services (such as email marketing platforms and analytics tools) to operate our website. These services have their own privacy policies and are selected for their compliance with applicable data protection laws.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
